This weeks food stop in Houston3/6/2015

This weeks stop was Eunice. Eunice is an upper class cajun resturant in th upper kirby area of Houston. It is to die for. The go to appetizer to start you off should be the Buratta and Biscuits with pepper jelly.

A Must have in Austin... TACO SHACK!!3/6/2015

This is a must go easy breakfast place. They serve all meals but the perfect breakfast taco is waiting for you. It is an easy location to get to and very fast and delicious.

Oysters to die for in Baton Rouge3/6/2015

This oyster bar is just downtown and is called Jolie Pearl. They have such great oyster with little to no grit, raw and cooked. They have such a good variety and so many differnt kinds which makes the experience a much better time. Where else can you get bacon and brie oysters... No where but here!!!
